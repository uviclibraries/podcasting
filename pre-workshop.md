## Pre-Workshop Videos & Activities
This workshop is primarily hands-on practice with Audacity in order to learn to use key features of the software. 

To participate fully, please do the following **before the workshop.**

1. Install Audacity on your laptop:
  - For Mac computers **[Click Here](https://www.audacityteam.org/download/mac/)** and choose either the Apple Silicon DMG installer [newer Macs](https://support.apple.com/en-us/116943) (IMPORTANT: choose the "Universal dmg" installer) or the Intel DMG installer [older Macs](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_Mac_models). 
  - For a Windows 64 bit computer **[Click Here](https://www.audacityteam.org/download/windows/)** and choose the appropriate installer for your system.
  - If you have a Windows ARM computer, which is quite unlikely in 2025, **[Click Here](https://github.com/audacity/audacity/releases/download/Audacity-3.7.5/audacity-win-3.7.5-arm64.exe)**.

2. Review the following videos: 
- [Should I Make a Podcast?](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zzLdlGDujZc) (4 min)

- [Planning your Podcast](podcast-planning.html) (5 min) 
- [Sample Podcast Script](sample-podcast-script.html) (2 min)
- OPTIONAL: [Podcasting 101 from PRX & Google Podcasts](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL7I-Eysv4kq4P9a_942suQdE1_0WiFxFo) is an excellent series of 10 short videos that cover the podcasting creation process from start to finish. They include a video on monetization, which is a topic not covered in this DSC workshop.
